Security Policy

Effective Date: July 31, 2024

1. Introduction

CivicLabs is committed to maintaining the security of its information systems and the data entrusted to us by our users. This Security Policy outlines the measures we take to safeguard your information.

2. Security Measures

2.1 Data Storage:

All data is stored in secure, access-controlled environments utilizing encryption and other security technologies to prevent unauthorized access.

2.2 Security Practices:

We implement standard industry security practices including, but not limited to, the use of Cloudflare Turnstile for enhanced security, regular code reviews, and ongoing vulnerability assessments.

3. Access Control

Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data based on their roles and responsibilities. Access rights are reviewed regularly to ensure compliance with our policies.

4. Incident Response

4.1 Detection and Response:

We regularly monitor for security breaches and have an incident response plan in place to address any security incidents rapidly.

4.2 Notification:

In the event of a data breach, affected parties will be notified within a reasonable timeframe, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Employee Training

All employees receive regular training on data security and privacy practices to ensure they are aware of and comply with our security policies.

6. Policy Review

This Security Policy is reviewed and updated at least annually, or as necessary, to adapt to any new security challenges or regulatory requirements.

7. Contact Us

For questions related to our Security Policy, please contact us at contact@civic-labs.ai.
