Open Digital Services. Ethical AI.

CivicLabs Alliance is a mission-driven joint venture, uniting Huntridge Labs and CivicActions to catalyze transformative change in digital government. As a SBA-certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) joint venture, we champion a vision of a more open, ethical, and effective digital world. Our passionate tech innovators create cutting-edge AI solutions and open source platforms that serve the greater good. Powered by a spirit of activism and unwavering commitment to ethical practices, CivicLabs is uniquely positioned to deliver secure, agile, and impactful solutions. We're not just building software; we're building a better digital future for all.
