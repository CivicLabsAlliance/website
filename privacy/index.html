We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and comply with industry norms.

You have the right to:

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, including Google Analytics, to enhance user experience and analyze website usage.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@civic-labs.ai.
